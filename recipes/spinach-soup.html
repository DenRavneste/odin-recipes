Spinach Soup (4p)


By tm under CC BY-SA 2.0

Description

It's spinach soup, innit.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1tblsp olive oil
  • 300g frozen spinach
  • 8dl water
  • 1 cube vegetable stock
  • 50g finely chopped leek
  • 1dl crème fraîche
  • 0.5tsp salt
  • 0.25tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs to your preference
  2. Chop the leek and add it to a hot pot with oil and thawed spinach, let it fry for a few minutes
  3. Add water with stock, and let boil for five minutes
  4. Add crème fraîche and eaven out the soup witha a mixer
  5. Add salt and pepper and serve with eggs
