Tea Eggs

Description

This is a recipe for Tea Eggs, which I have simplified a a little from the original. It's a very longwinded process so I recommend making a big batch and saving this whole project for special occasions. The picture above is of pickled eggs technically, so the final dish will not look exactly like that.

Ingredients

  • As many eggs as you wish to make
  • Enough water to cover the eggs
  • 2tsps of sugar
  • 2tsps of salt
  • 2 star star anise
  • 1 stick of cinnamon
  • Plenty of soy sauce
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1tblsp of cayenne powder

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs for five minutes in just the water
  2. Remove them and use a spoon to gently crack their shell all around, without removing the shell
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients, bring back to a boil, and add the eggs
  4. Boil for 20 minutes, then let it cool for 20 minutes
  5. Simmer for 1 hour
  6. Let cool for 1 hour
  7. Place in the fridge over night
  8. Next morning, you can remove the eggs from the brew if you'd like. The eggs are now ready
